TechVision Inc is a software company based in Austin.
Rahul Sharma is the CEO of TechVision Inc.
Priya Nair is the CTO of TechVision Inc.
Arjun Mehta is the CFO of TechVision Inc.
Priya Nair manages the Engineering Team at TechVision Inc.
Arjun Mehta manages the Finance Team at TechVision Inc.

TechVision Inc partnered with CloudNova Ltd in January 2025.
CloudNova Ltd supplies cloud servers to TechVision Inc.
TechVision Inc signed a contract with PaySafe Corp in February 2025.
PaySafe Corp provides payment services to TechVision Inc.

Priya Nair developed a product called VisionAI in 2024.
VisionAI automates data analysis for enterprise clients.
Rahul Sharma approved VisionAI and presented it to the Board.
Arjun Mehta allocated 1 million dollars to fund VisionAI.

A data breach occurred at TechVision Inc in March 2025.
The data breach affected the client database.
Priya Nair detected the data breach and reported it to Rahul Sharma.
Rahul Sharma hired SecureNet LLC to resolve the data breach.
SecureNet LLC is a cybersecurity firm based in Dallas.
SecureNet LLC fixed the data breach by April 2025.

TechVision Inc acquired DataEdge startup in May 2025.
DataEdge specializes in real-time analytics.
Rahul Sharma announced the DataEdge acquisition to the Board.
Arjun Mehta approved 2 million dollars for the DataEdge acquisition.
Priya Nair integrated DataEdge technology into VisionAI.
